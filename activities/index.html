Team Building Activities

At GAIR Lab, we believe that fostering a strong team culture is essential for collaborative research and innovation. Our team regularly engages in various activities to strengthen bonds, celebrate achievements, and create a supportive work environment.
  • Team Outings: We organize regular outings to places like Disneyland to enjoy time together outside the lab.
  • Group Dinners: We celebrate research milestones, paper acceptances, and special occasions with team dinners.
